# MiroTalk on EvoPlat

![evoplat](../images/evoplat.png)

[EvoPlat](https://evoplat.com) is a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform that offers a range of integrated services and features, including MiroTalk as a plugin for secure real-time audio/video communication and collaboration.

### Why MiroTalk + EvoPlat

With EvoPlat you can use all the MiroTalk versions without the need to deploy them on your own servers for your video conferences. Through a simple monthly or lifetime subscription, MiroTalk is delivered as a ready-to-use, fully managed plugin that is always kept up to date.

### Key Benefits

*  **No self-hosting required** – Use MiroTalk without deploying or maintaining your own servers.
*  **All MiroTalk versions included** – Access every MiroTalk edition directly from the platform.
*  **Flexible subscriptions** – Choose a monthly or lifetime plan that fits your needs.
*  **Always up to date** – Benefit from automatic updates and the latest features without manual maintenance.
*  **Integrated SaaS ecosystem** – Combine MiroTalk with EvoPlat's other integrated services and features.

### Get Started

MiroTalk is available as a plugin on EvoPlat. For more information about the platform and its capabilities, visit the [official website](https://evoplat.com).
