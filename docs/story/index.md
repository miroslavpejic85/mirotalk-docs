# 🎬 The Story Behind MiroTalk

---

🌍 **The world changed overnight.**

When the global pandemic hit, video communication went from a convenience to a **lifeline**. Like millions of others, I found myself glued to screens, trying to stay connected with friends, family, and collaborators through online meeting platforms.

But something didn't feel right. ⚠️

---

## 🔒 The Problem No One Was Talking About

Most of the available solutions were controlled by **massive corporations**, closed ecosystems where users had **little to no control** over their own data. Privacy? It felt like an afterthought. Vendor lock-in made it nearly impossible for individuals and organizations to truly **own** their communication infrastructure.

> 💡 *"Why should we hand over our most private conversations to companies that treat our data as a product?"*

That question kept me up at night. And that's when **everything changed.**

---

## 🚀 A Bold Idea Takes Shape

I decided to build something different. Something **radical** in its simplicity:

A **free, open-source WebRTC platform** that anyone, anywhere in the world, could deploy on their own server. No strings attached. No hidden data collection. No corporate gatekeepers.

**The mission was crystal clear:**

- 🚫 **No vendor lock-in**: your platform, your rules
- 🛡️ **Full control over your data**: because privacy is a right, not a feature
- 🔐 **Privacy by design**: built into every line of code
- 🌐 **Open source & accessible to everyone**: free as in freedom

---

## 💬 And the Name?

Friends and colleagues have always called me **"Miro."** Since the project was all about enabling people to communicate **freely and fearlessly**, the name came naturally:

> ### ✨ **Miro + Talk = MiroTalk**

A simple name. A powerful purpose. Open communication built with a **personal touch** and a whole lot of heart. ❤️

---

## 🌟 From Experiment to Movement

What started as a **small experiment during lockdown** quickly grew into something far bigger than I ever imagined. Today, MiroTalk is used by people **all around the world**:

- 👨‍💻 **Developers** who value open-source principles
- 🎓 **Educators** connecting with students across borders
- 👥 **Teams** who refuse to compromise on privacy
- 🏘️ **Communities** who believe communication tools should **empower users, not control them**

---
